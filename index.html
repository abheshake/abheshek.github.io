ABOUT

I'm a cinematographer who specializes in creating visually compelling narratives that resonate with audiences. With professional training from FACE – Film and Television Academy, Mumbai, I combine technical mastery with creative vision.

Every frame is an opportunity to tell a story. Through strategic lighting, precise composition, and innovative camera movement, I transform concepts into cinematic experiences that leave lasting impressions.

My work spans commercial campaigns, narrative features, personal projects, and photography. Each project is approached with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to visual excellence.
